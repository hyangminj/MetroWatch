MetroWatch Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 28, 2025

1. Overview

MetroWatch ("the App") is a metronome application for Wear OS (Galaxy Watch). This Privacy Policy describes how the App handles your information.

2. Information We Collect

This App does not collect any personal information.

Settings such as BPM, time signature, and volume are stored only on your device and are never transmitted to external servers.

3. Use of Information

Since we do not collect any personal information, there is no information to use.

4. Sharing of Information

Since we do not collect any personal information, there is no information to share with third parties.

5. App Permissions

This App uses the following permissions:

  • Vibration (VIBRATE): Used to provide haptic feedback synchronized with the beat.

This permission is used solely for metronome functionality and is not related to personal data collection.

6. Data Security

All app settings are stored locally on your device only. The App does not use network communication, eliminating any risk of data leakage.

7. Children's Privacy

Since this App does not collect any personal information, it is safe for users of all ages, including children.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
