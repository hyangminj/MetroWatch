# MetroWatch v1.0 - Release Notes (English)

## Release Notes

**Welcome to MetroWatch v1.0!**

We're excited to bring you the first official release of MetroWatch - a precision metronome designed for your wrist.

---

## What's New

### Core Features
- **Flexible BPM Control**: Adjust tempo from 40 to 240 BPM with 1 BPM precision
- **Multiple Time Signatures**: Support for 2/4, 3/4, 4/4, and 6/8 time signatures
- **Dual Feedback System**: Independent control over sound and vibration with 0-100% volume sliders
- **Accent Beats**: Stronger vibration and higher pitch on the first beat of each measure
- **Real-time Beat Counter**: Visual indication of current beat position in the measure

### User Interface
- Optimized for round Wear OS watch faces
- Clean, scrollable layout for easy navigation
- Dark theme for improved battery efficiency
- Large, readable beat counter display
- One-tap time signature switching

### Performance
- Lightweight (~6MB) with minimal battery impact
- No internet connection required
- No ads or tracking
- Smooth 60+ FPS performance on Galaxy Watch

## Quick Tips
- **Silent Practice**: Use vibration-only mode to practice without disturbing others
- **Group Sessions**: Use sound-only mode to keep your group synchronized
- **Quick Changes**: Tap the time signature display to cycle through available signatures

## Technical Details
- Minimum Android version: Android 11 (API 30)
- Target Android version: Android 14 (API 34)
- Compatible with: Galaxy Watch 4, Watch 4 Classic, Watch 5, Watch 5 Pro, and newer Wear OS devices
- No special permissions required
